Iframe Embedding Test

Verifies that the Solar System Explorer works correctly when embedded.

Manual Test Checklist:

  • Drag to rotate the view
  • Pinch/scroll to zoom in and out
  • Tap/click a planet to see its fact card
  • Use the time slider at the bottom
  • Press Space to pause/play
  • Scroll events stay inside the iframe (no page scroll)
  • Touch events work on mobile (pinch zoom, two-finger rotate, tap)
  • Sound button works (browser autoplay policy)
  • Postcard capture button works
